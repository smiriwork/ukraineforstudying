Our mission

Our team will put you on the track of your post-bachelor adventure, thanks to our experience we will guide you and support you throughout the process of obtaining the invitation letter, visa and a in the university of your dreams.

The required documents to obtain the invitation letter are; a copy of the baccalaureate, a transcript of records and a copy of the passport.

Our Services

Invitation letter

We will take care of the deposit by hand of your pre-registration file at the chosen university. Thanks to our experience and contacts you will be provided with the mandatory letter of invitation to obtain a student visa for Ukraine

Visa

We will start with you the administrative formalities for obtaining the student visa, providing you with the exact information and indicating the necessary steps to obtain it

Transport

Upon arrival at the Kiev International Airport, you will be welcomed and accompanied to your university residence by one of our team members

Registration

Once you arrive to your destination, you will be guided during the process of your final registration

Housing

You will have a guaranteed accommodation in the university campus during your first school year

Integration and support

We also offer you the possibility of putting you in touch with other students residing in the same city through social meetings

Vinnytsia

In this stage, you face an endless number of choices. Regarding the choice of city and university, Kiev and Járkov are presented as the most common choice among students, and our team can get you an invitation letter Of both universities and any other.

But all the same, we want to present you an alternative, a small gem of city and university, its name is Vinnytsia. At two hours from Kiev, and 370 thousand inhabitants, Vinnytsia presents itself as a small town, quiet, not expensive At all and very safe. One can do everything and find everything there, but in what it excels the most, it is to education, it shelters one of the best universities of the country, and the best in the medical field (medicine , Dentistry and pharmacy), we encourage you to keep it in mind
